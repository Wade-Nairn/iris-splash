Overview

Background

The Availability Calendar (aCal) is an interim tool to manage NEO employees’ availability during the planning for, and response to, emergencies.

Who Can Use aCal?

aCal can be used by any registered FireWeb user: with an emergency role; who is a manager; who is a Duty Officer; or who is a Resources Officer.

Why Use aCal?

aCal allows visibility of personnel across the Department and has many uses, including:
  • individuals flagging their heightened availability, or unavailability;
  • supporting the development of rosters and shift plans;
  • searching for available staff with particular roles;
  • identifying ‘business critical’ staff;
  • managers viewing their direct reports’ availability;
  • reporting functions.

What Does ‘Availability’ Mean?

Being available means you are not: on leave; taking time-in- lieu; deployed; on rest days associated with deployments.
