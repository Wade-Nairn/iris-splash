Overview

The IRIS Shift and Resource Planner (ShaRP) is a web application developed, largely, for NSW Parks and Wildlife.
It closely integrates with IRIS and provides for Tour Of Duty management and interaction with Resource Requesting.
It is a version 1 product and will require additional enhancement in the future, but is a good fit for DELWP's business.

ShaRP has been demonstrated to regional DELWP staff and has received overwhelming interest and support.

Benefits

Provides a quick road into:

  • Shift planning and shift change at an incident
  • Tour of Duty rotation planning
  • Standby rosters, and
  • Burn Operations planning.

Opens the door to future adoption of, and system support for:

  • Resource estimation for Prescribed Burning
  • Standby, and other short, medium and long roster management
  • Integrated Resource Requesting - linking ShaRP to SRRS and IRIS Coordination
  • Processes for capture of 'pre-incident' work
  • Reduction of the usage of IRIS for non-real-time data capture - Stop recording Standby in IRIS Coordination
  • Production of Incident Action Plans and Deployment orders, and
  • Forward looking Availability view
